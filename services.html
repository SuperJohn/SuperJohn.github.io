EV Charging Installation

Built for daily home charging: clean, planned Level 2 EV charger installations for homeowners who want reliable home charging with the electrical capacity reviewed before work begins.

Electrical Panel Upgrades

Panel upgrades are evaluated when the EV charger requires more capacity than the existing panel can safely support.

Not every EV charger installation requires a panel upgrade. We review the existing electrical panel, available capacity, charger amperage, existing home loads, and installation conditions before recommending additional infrastructure.

Panel capacity and load review
Residential panel replacement planning
Infrastructure planning for future residential power demand

Generator Backup

Generator backup services are available for homeowners planning outage mitigation and critical power support.

Generator backup work is positioned as a secondary service after EV charging and panel upgrade work. The focus is on planned residential power resilience, not emergency service calls.

Whole-home standby generator installation planning
Automatic transfer switch integration
Critical load panel isolation
Backup power planning for outage mitigation

Battery Backup

Battery backup services are available for homeowners planning longer-term energy resilience and smarter home power management.

Battery backup is treated as a broader residential power upgrade service. It may be considered where homeowners want backup capability, public safety power shutoff resilience, or energy management support.

Home battery backup planning
Tesla Powerwall-style and Enphase-style system support
Backup circuit planning
Smart energy management and peak usage considerations
